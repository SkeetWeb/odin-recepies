Meat Pie Recipe

Description

Create a savory delight with this Classic Beef and Mushroom Meat Pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 cup diced mushrooms
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 package refrigerated pie crusts (2 crusts)

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef in a skillet, then drain excess fat.
  2. Sauté onions and mushrooms until tender.
  3. Stir in the meat mixture.
  4. Roll out pie crusts; place one in a greased dish, add the filling, cover with the second crust.
  5. Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 30-35 minutes. Enjoy your delicious
