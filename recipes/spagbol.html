Spag bol aka Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Create an Australian household staple, juicy mince covered in spaghetti pasta and Bolognese Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 8 oz (225g) spaghetti
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes

Steps

  1. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat, breaking it into crumbles.
  2. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, cooking until onions are translucent.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, stirring well. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  5. Serve the bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Optionally, garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil. Enjoy your quick and delicious spaghetti bolognese!
